The Clinical Procedure

Sapphire micro-channels combined with direct Choi Pen implantation.

1

3D Hairline Design

Computer-assisted facial mapping to design the most natural and aesthetic hairline.

2

Comfort-In Anesthesia

Needle-free, painless local anesthesia applied using high-pressure micro-spray technology.

3

Precise Extraction

Healthy grafts are harvested individually from the donor area using micro-motors.

4

Direct Implantation

Using the Choi Pen, grafts are implanted directly at the perfect angle and depth without prior incisions.

5

Density Optimization

Strategic placement ensures maximum density and natural flow of the hair.

6

PRP Therapy Application

Platelet-Rich Plasma is applied to accelerate healing and boost graft survival to 99%.
